Cherry Pineapple Bologna

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup crushed pineapple
  • 1/4 cup coarsely chopped maraschino cherries
  • 1/4 cup light corn syrup
  • 2 tabelspoons white vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cloves
  • 2 drops red food color
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons water
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cornstarch

Preparation

  1. arrange bologna in ungreased 9-inch pie pan

  2. Cut ring diagonally at 2-inch intervalsbeing careful not to cut completely through

  3. Separate cuts and spoon in part of glaze.

  4. Spread remaining glaze on top.

  5. Heat oven to 400Â°

  6. Prepare Potato Buds instant puffs as directed on package

  7. Mount potatoes in center of ring

  8. Bake 20 minutes or until potatoes are light brown. 4 servings.
